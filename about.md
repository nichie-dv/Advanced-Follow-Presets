# Advanced Follow Presets
Tired of having to redo the same advanced follow triggers over and over? Premade settings just not doing it for you? Well look no further.
This mod is an overhaul for advanced follow premade settings.


## How to use
Advanced Follow Presets adds more functionality to that nifty little window in the top right corner of the advanced follow trigger.

The plus button adds a new preset with the current settings, while the x button will delete presets.

You can customize your presets by picking the color of the icon for better organization.


## Extras
This mod also comes with customizable folder paths, a template (for scripting perhaps), and wokemode.

If you delete the base robtop presets, fret not, for they and the blank template are regenerated on mod load when they are not found.
